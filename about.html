About Fine Reading Club:


Fine Reading Book Club was founded by a group of people who had
similar understanding and knowledge of the importance and positive effects of reading.
Prior to the founding of the club, the fouders used to get together on a regular basis to discuss books
and share their reading experiences.
Subsquently, they had come up with the decision to establish the book club. By doing so, they created a place where
people can come and enjoy reading books, share experiences, and get courage to read a variety of books, making friends,
having fun and meeting like-mined people.
